Where to Stay

On-site at Ashokan Center

As we party late into Saturday night, we want you to be able to stagger into bed as late as you wish. There are enough beds for the entire wedding party.

Beds at Ashokan are humble. While the mattresses are thick and comfortable, it's like upscale summer camp. Most beds are bunks.

If you'd like to stay on-site, please indicate this in your RSVP form and we'll be in touch with more details.

Room options

Ashokan offers four types of rooms. Please note that due to capacity of the rooms below, unless your wedding party has 3, 4, or 5 people (including children), you will likely need to share a room with fellow guests. Don't worry, we'll help you to find a great match! We encourage you to establish room blocks with mutual friends yourselves. Otherwise, we'll assist you in finding comfortable room assignments with fellow guests.

All arrangements cost $55-$75 per person per night.

Small Rooms with Queen Bunk and Twin Bunk

There are eight rooms at Ashokan with one queen bunk (queen on the bottom, twin on top) and one twin bunk. The bathroom will be private. Sleeps 5.

Small Rooms with two Twin Bunks

There are four rooms with two twin bunk beds (four twin beds total). The bathroom will be private. Sleeps 4.

Small Rooms with Queen Bunk

There are four rooms with one queen bunk (queen on the bottom, twin on top). The bathroom will be shared. Sleeps 3.

Dormitory Rooms

Ashokan has 3 large dormitory rooms that have 12 bunk beds each. We know you've developed higher standards of privacy since the days of summer camp. But how much time will you spend in your room anyways? The bathroom will be shared.

For those who would like to camp, Ashokan offers space for camping with access to a bathhouse for $16 per person per night.

Learn more about lodging at Ashokan on their lodging page.
