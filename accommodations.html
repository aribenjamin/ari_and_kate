Where to Stay

On-site at Ashokan Center

We recommend you stay with us at the Ashokan Center. As we party into the wee hours on Saturday night, we want you to be able to stagger into bed at any late time of your choosing. There are enough beds for the entire wedding party.

Beds at Ashokan are humble. While the mattresses are thick and comfortable, it's like upscale summer camp. Most beds are bunks.

Private Rooms

There are 20 private rooms at Ashokan. Eight of these have queen bunks (queen on the bottom, twin on top). The other 12 rooms have two twin bunk beds. All rooms have their own bathroom.

Ashokan dorms

Ashokan has 3 large dormitory rooms that have 12 bunk beds each. Combined, these rooms can sleep 72 people. We know you've developed higher standards of privacy since the days of summer camp. BUT how much time will you spend in your room anyways?

If you'd like to stay on-site, please indicate this in your RSVP form and we'll be in touch with more details.

Vacation Rentals

There are many vacation rentals (Airbnb, VRBO) available in the surrounding areas of Woodstock, Phoenicia, and Kingston. We recommend booking early as accommodations fill up quickly in this popular area.

Transportation

We will provide shuttle service between select local hotels and the venue for the wedding events. Details will be provided closer to the wedding date.
