Where to Stay

On-site at Ashokan Center

As we party into late into Saturday night, we want you to be able to stagger into bed as late as you wish. There are enough beds for the entire wedding party.

Beds at Ashokan are humble. While the mattresses are thick and comfortable, it's like upscale summer camp. Most beds are bunks.

If you'd like to stay on-site, please indicate this in your RSVP form (expect an email/card) and we'll be in touch with more details.

Private Rooms with Queen Bunks

There are 8 private rooms at Ashokan with queen bunks (queen on the bottom, twin on top). Perfect for couples or small families.

Private Rooms with Twin Bunks

There are 12 private rooms with two twin bunk beds (four twin beds total). These rooms are ideal for families or friends who want to share a room.

Dormitory Rooms

Ashokan has 3 large dormitory rooms that have 12 bunk beds each. We know you've developed higher standards of privacy since the days of summer camp. But how much time will you spend in your room anyways?

Learn more about lodging at Ashokan on their lodging page.

Nearby Hotels

We recognize that some guests will want more privacy and comfort than Ashokan has to offer. There are a range of luxury accommodations that can be found within a 30-minute drive of the venue.

We will be providing a Saturday night shuttle service to hotels and B&B's in Woodstock.
