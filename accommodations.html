Where to Stay

Here are some options for your stay during our wedding weekend.

On-site at Ashokan Center

The Ashokan Center offers on-site lodging in their comfortable cabins and lodges. These accommodations are limited and will be assigned on a first-come, first-served basis.

If you'd like to stay on-site, please indicate this in your RSVP form and we'll be in touch with more details.

Vacation Rentals

There are many vacation rentals (Airbnb, VRBO) available in the surrounding areas of Woodstock, Phoenicia, and Kingston. We recommend booking early as accommodations fill up quickly in this popular area.

Transportation

We will provide shuttle service between select local hotels and the venue for the wedding events. Details will be provided closer to the wedding date.

Directions to the Venue

From New York City:

  1. Take the NYS Thruway (I-87) North to Exit 19 (Kingston)
  2. Follow NY-28 West for about 10 miles
  3. Turn right onto County Route 42/Oliverea Road
  4. Continue for about 4 miles, then turn left onto Beaverkill Road
  5. The Ashokan Center will be on your right after about 0.5 miles
