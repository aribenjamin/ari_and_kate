Ashokan Center

The Ashokan Center is a beautiful venue located in the Catskill Mountains of New York. With its stunning natural surroundings and rustic charm, it's the perfect setting for Kate and Ari's wedding celebration.

About the Venue

The Ashokan Center is a 385-acre outdoor education, conference, and retreat center. It features beautiful hiking trails, a waterfall, and historic buildings set in the picturesque Catskill Mountains.

Getting There

The Ashokan Center is approximately 2 hours from New York City. It is accessible by car, and there are also bus services to nearby Kingston, NY. For those flying in, the nearest airports are Stewart International Airport (SWF) and Albany International Airport (ALB).

Venue Amenities

The venue offers a range of amenities including:

  • On-site lodging options
  • Multiple event spaces for ceremony and reception
  • Beautiful outdoor spaces
  • Hiking trails and nature walks
  • Full-service dining facilities
